# DESIGN SYSTEM

## Guiding Principle

TSRAONE Gateway should feel like a luminous architectural system, not a flat collection of UI cards.

The design language is based on:

- daylight
- air
- glass
- depth
- restraint

## Visual Foundation

### Base Palette

Primary material space:

- warm white
- pearl white
- pale silver

Secondary atmospheric accents:

- soft sky blue
- faint cyan
- pale violet
- subtle champagne warmth

The base environment must remain bright. Accent color should appear as optical diffusion, not decorative paint.

## Sunlight System

The environment is driven by a directional sunlight source rather than a generic background gradient.

Desired qualities:

- brighter side of the scene
- believable falloff across space
- soft bloom and light spread
- visible hierarchy between direct and ambient illumination

The scene should suggest sunlight entering a glass pavilion from an architectural opening.

## Atmosphere

Atmosphere is critical to avoiding flatness.

Required atmospheric qualities:

- fine dust motes in light
- soft haze
- depth-separated luminance
- subtle structural planes in the background

These layers should support scale and air, not become decorative noise.

## Glass Material Language

Glass must feel thin, precise, luminous, and engineered.

Each premium glass surface should include:

1. controlled translucency
2. soft but restrained blur
3. crisp edge highlight
4. subtle interior light gradient
5. reflective / specular response
6. cursor-reactive highlight movement
7. layered depth against the environment

Avoid:

- muddy gray cards
- generic frosted dashboard panels
- thick opaque blur blocks

## Composition Rules

The portal should privilege one strong spatial scene over many weak blocks.

Composition targets:

- generous negative space
- clear focal hierarchy
- large central portal core
- elegant placement for `test2`, `test`, `aitrader`, `connect`, `note`, and the root-domain node
- minimal but high-quality supporting text

Avoid:

- dashboard grids dominating the experience
- rectangular block stacks
- template-style hero patterns
- overuse of pills and chips

## Typography

Typography should feel premium, quiet, and assured.

Rules:

- modern sans-serif
- strong hero scale
- tight but clean tracking
- dark text on bright space
- supporting copy kept concise

Tone should remain:

- confident
- minimal
- precise
- slightly futuristic

## Motion Relationship To Design

Motion is part of the material system.

It should:

- reinforce depth
- reinforce focus
- reinforce glass realism
- reinforce spatial hierarchy

It should never exist as a detached layer of ornament.
