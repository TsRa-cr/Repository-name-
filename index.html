Interaction Layers

Every response shares the same motion grammar.

Mouse Movement

Depth and light glide behind the cursor on a spring.

Foreground surfaces answer quickly, background fields drift more slowly, and highlights never snap to position.

Hover

Magnetic attraction adds lift without losing control.

Hovered elements scale softly, bias toward the cursor, and push ambient color and particle density around them.

Click

Compression, convergence, then a camera-like transition.

Press states are tactile first; energy effects and portal zoom only appear once intent is clear.

Scroll

Sections evolve as a scene, not as stacked rectangles.

Inertial rendering smooths the page while each section shifts layout, glow, and object depth based on progress.
